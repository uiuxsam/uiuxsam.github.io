My Process

    - Results focused not processes
    - Always people and interactions over processes and tools
    - I help Product Owners visualize their products

The process varies depending on several factors like - the business goals and requirements, user needs, budget and time, internal or B2B or B2C product, agile or waterfall, onsite or off shore team, time difference, team size, and people working on the project and so on.
