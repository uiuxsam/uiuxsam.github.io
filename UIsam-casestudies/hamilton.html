The City of Hamilton, a rust belt city, is centrally located between the Cincinnati and Dayton metropolitan areas in southeast Ohio. The city's economy was gradually declining for last few decades. However, the latest city administration planned to put significant amount of effort to improve the city's economy by using the advantages that it is an important regional center of business, industry, culture, and government. To showcase all these, the city needed a website solely for economic development purpose besides the main city website.
