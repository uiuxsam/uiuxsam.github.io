Freddie Mac Loan Advisor Suite


• Applications display: The number of tools/applications keep increasing. It became particularly challenging to show all of them along with the icons, label and tagline without having the user scroll down. Organizing them horizontally in two rows solved the issue.

• Login: Forgot User Name and Password used to be below the respective form fields and it used to take more space vertically. Putting those links together below the Login button worked better.

• Carousel: It was very challenging to show the updates and news in the previous version of the page. Using carousels in the new design solved the Issue.

• Feedback button: This button used to be at the bottom of the page and out of the users sight unless they scroll down. Now the button is static at the same position on the right side of the page.

Loan Advisor Suite is an end-to-end technology solution to provide greater certainty through insight into quality, risk and alignment with eligibility requirements, which helps to originate and deliver loans to Freddie Mac more efficiently. The tools in the Suite are: Loan Product Advisor, Loan Collateral Advisor, Loan Quality Advisor, Loan Closing Advisor, Selling System, Loan Coverage Advisor, Quality Control Advisor and Business Intelligence.
