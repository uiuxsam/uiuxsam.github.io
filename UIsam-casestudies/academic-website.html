Website for Academics


Generally users find this lab website boring and monotonous. There are many design problems with this website. Some of them are -

• The navigation bar has too many tabs which causes cognitive strain to the user

• The selected tab looks almost similar to unselected ones. It should be significantly different

• The hero image area is taking huge amount of space and its the same for every page. As a result the user has to scroll down to see the content

• There are multiple different types of fonts used in different purposes

• Some of the font colors fails to maintain contrast

• Overwhelming amount of text in each tab





• Navigation bar: There are 10 tabs and all of them are important. However, many of them can be sub-menu under specific items. And the tabs can be minimized to 6.

• Selected tab: It looks almost similar to unselected ones. The issue is fixed with visible difference between them.

• Hero image: It has a lot of important info but is taking a huge amount of space and its the same for every page. However, users look for those info in other tabs like About or Contacts. Therefore the hero image is removed and the info is put in the Contacts tab.

• Fonts: Fonts are used in a imbalanced way with different sizes and colors. As a solution, a single font has been used with balanced contrast.

• Text: The text is reduced by using second level tabs and accordions.

• Scroling: Too much scrolling in long pages.

ANALYTICS

Research Tab: Research, Opportunities and Members were taking too much space in the navigation bar in the current website. All these categories are research related and can be put under Research. In the proposed design, Research Projects, Research Team members and Research Opportunities are inside Research tab as secondary tabs. Opportunities for different types of interested parties info is inside accordions which reduces scrolling and helps users to focus on what they are looking for.

Publications Tab: In the current website, publications and conferences are 2 tabs in the navigation bar. All these categories are publication related and can be put under Publication. In the proposed design, publications are categorized in 4 different types along with number of publication of respective types.

About Tab: In the current website, there is no About tab in the navigation bar. However, there is a tab called Teaching which means courses taught by the site owner. In the proposed design, About tab has 4 different secondary tabs on Courses taught, Educational achievements, work Experience and Awards won.

Miscellaneous Tab: In the current website, there is no Miscellaneous tab in the navigation bar. Instead, there are tabs called News and Gallery. In the proposed design, Miscellaneous tab has 4 different secondary tabs on Photo Gallery, Blogs by the writer, News and Past Projects.
